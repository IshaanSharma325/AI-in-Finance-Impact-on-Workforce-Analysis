# AI-in-Finance-Impact-on-Workforce-Analysis
This repository hosts a comprehensive analysis of Artificial Intelligence's (AI) transformative impact on the financial sector, with a specific focus on Personal Financial Advisors and Financial Analysts & Risk Specialists. Developed as a college research project, this notebook integrates synthesized data from reputable sources like the US Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), Morgan Stanley, McKinsey, Gartner, BCG, PwC, and the World Economic Forum.

Project Scope:

AI Application: Examining AI's role in data analysis and future stock price projections.
Workforce Impact: Assessing the effects on financial advisors and analysts, including job creation, efficiency gains, and displacement.
Key Metrics: Analyzing historical job baselines, projected growth, efficiency improvements, job reshaping, and new role trends.
Contents: This repository includes an interactive Jupyter Notebook (Google Colab compatible) containing:

Data Loading & Preprocessing: Python code for loading and structuring the synthesized data.
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Visualizations illustrating job trends, efficiency impacts, and workforce transformations.
Research Article Outline: A structured markdown article, serving as a template for a college research paper, complete with an introduction, methodology, detailed analysis sections, conclusions, and MLA-formatted references.
This project aims to provide a data-driven perspective on the dual nature of AI—as both a disruptive and an enabling force—in shaping the future of financial professions.
